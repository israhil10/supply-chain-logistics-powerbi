# Project Overview

The **Supply Chain Logistics Performance Dashboard** is a data analytics project built using **Power BI** to analyze logistics performance, delivery efficiency, and shipment delays.

The dashboard provides insights into key operational metrics such as:

- Total shipments
- Average delivery time
- On-time delivery rate
- Delayed shipment percentage
- Route-level delivery delays
- Cutoff time impact on delivery performance

The goal of this project is to help logistics teams **monitor operational efficiency and identify delivery bottlenecks across routes and time periods.**

---

# Business Problem

Supply chain and logistics operations often face challenges such as:

- Shipment delivery delays  
- Inefficient transportation routes  
- Cutoff time constraints affecting dispatch operations  
- Limited visibility into logistics performance  

Without proper analytics, logistics managers cannot easily identify where delays occur or which routes require optimization.

This project solves that problem by providing **interactive dashboards that highlight delivery performance and operational inefficiencies.**

---

# Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

- Analyze shipment delivery performance  
- Measure **on-time vs delayed deliveries**  
- Identify **routes with the highest delivery delays**  
- Track **daily delivery delay trends**  
- Understand the impact of **cutoff times on delivery delays**  
- Provide **interactive visualizations for logistics decision-making**

# Dataset Description

- **Domain:** Supply Chain / Logistics  
- **Dataset File:** `delivery_data.csv`  
- **Data Type:** Shipment and delivery tracking data  

### Key Fields

The dataset includes logistics information such as:

- Shipment ID  
- Route / Delivery Hub  
- Shipment Date  
- Delivery Date  
- Delivery Time  
- Cutoff Time  
- Delivery Delay  
- On-Time Delivery Status  
- Route Type  

This data enables analysis of **delivery efficiency, route performance, and logistics delays.**

# Dashboard Components

## KPI Metrics

The dashboard includes the following key performance indicators:

- **Total Shipments**
- **Average Delivery Time**
- **On-Time Deliveries (%)**
- **Delayed Shipments (%)**

These KPIs provide an **instant overview of logistics performance.**

---

## Top Routes with Highest Delivery Delay

This visualization identifies **routes that experience the most delays**, allowing logistics teams to focus on improving problematic routes.

---

## Cutoff Time Analysis

This chart analyzes the percentage of shipments that meet or miss **cutoff times**, helping identify operational inefficiencies in dispatch timing.

---

## Average Delivery Delay Trend (Daily)

Shows how delivery delays change over time, helping detect **performance trends and operational disruptions.**

---

## Average Delay vs Cutoff by Hour

This analysis compares **average delivery delay and cutoff shipments across different hours of the day**, providing insights into how operational timing impacts delivery performance.

---

# Key Insights

From the dashboard analysis:

- Over **72% of deliveries are completed on time**
- Approximately **27% of shipments experience delays**
- Certain routes consistently show **higher delivery delays**
- Delivery delays fluctuate across **different times of the day**
- Cutoff times significantly influence shipment delivery performance
